# SnapRecipe Mobile App Support

**Last Updated:** April 16, 2026

Welcome to SnapRecipe Support! We're here to help you get the most out of your AI-powered recipe companion.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How does SnapRecipe work?
SnapRecipe uses advanced artificial intelligence to analyze photos of food and generate a recipe. Simply snap a picture or import one from your photo library, and our AI will identify the dish, suggest ingredients, and provide step-by-step cooking instructions.

### What kind of photos work best?
For the best results, try to use clear, well-lit photos where the dish is centered and fills most of the frame. Even lighting helps our AI accurately identify ingredients and suggest a recipe.

### Can I set dietary preferences?
Yes! You can set your dietary preferences in the app's settings. These preferences will help SnapRecipe tailor recipes and ingredient substitutions to your specific needs (e.g., Vegan, Gluten-Free).

### Where are my saved recipes stored?
Your saved recipes are stored locally on your device. This means your personal recipe collection is always available to you, even offline, and is not stored on our servers.

### How many free scans do I get?
SnapRecipe offers a limited number of free scans per month. You can track your remaining scans in the app. A premium upgrade option will be available in the future for unlimited scans and other benefits.

### I'm having trouble generating a recipe. What should I do?
If a recipe cannot be generated, please try again with a different photo. Ensure the image is clear and that the food is easily identifiable. If the issue persists, please contact support.
