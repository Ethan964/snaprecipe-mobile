# SnapRecipe Mobile App Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** April 16, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how SnapRecipe Mobile App ("SnapRecipe," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and discloses information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

## 1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide and improve our App and its features. The types of information we collect include:

*   **Food Photos:** When you use the App's camera feature or import photos from your library, we collect the food images you submit. These images are used to generate recipes using artificial intelligence.
*   **Dietary Preferences:** You may optionally provide dietary preferences (e.g., Vegan, Gluten-Free). These preferences are used to personalize recipe suggestions and substitutions.
*   **Usage Data:** We collect data related to your interaction with the App, such as the number of recipes generated (scan usage), saved recipes, and notification preferences. This data helps us understand how the App is used and improve its functionality.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

*   **Recipe Generation:** Food photos and dietary preferences are sent to our AI backend to analyze the dish and generate a recipe, including ingredients, instructions, and tips.
*   **Personalization:** Dietary preferences are used to tailor recipe suggestions and substitutions to your needs.
*   **App Functionality:** Usage data, such as saved recipes and scan counts, is used to manage your in-app experience, including free scan limits and the display of saved content.
*   **Improvement of Services:** We may use aggregated and anonymized data to understand user behavior and improve the AI recipe generation and overall App experience.

## 3. How We Store Your Information

*   **Local Storage:** Your dietary preferences, saved recipes, and monthly scan usage data are stored locally on your device using `AsyncStorage`. This means your personal recipe data is not stored on our servers unless explicitly shared by you.
*   **Image Processing:** Food photos are temporarily sent to our tRPC backend for AI processing to generate recipes. We do not permanently store these images on our servers after recipe generation.

## 4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes. We may disclose information in the following circumstances:

*   **AI Backend:** Food photos and dietary preferences are shared with our AI backend for the sole purpose of generating recipes. This backend processes the data to provide the core functionality of the App.
*   **Legal Requirements:** We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., a court order or government agency).

## 5. Your Choices and Rights

*   **Dietary Preferences:** You can modify your dietary preferences at any time within the App's settings.
*   **Notifications:** You can enable or disable notifications for scan reset reminders and saved recipe prompts in the App's settings.
*   **Saved Recipes:** You can save and remove recipes locally on your device.
*   **Data Access:** As your data is primarily stored locally, you have direct control over it through the App's features.

## 6. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information.

## 7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
